💡 Quick Reminder – Simple Present (positive)
▼

✅ I / you / we / they

Just use the verb:
I play. · We live here.
They walk to school.

⚠️ He / she / it → +s

Add -s to the verb:
He plays. · She likes cats.

⚠️ Special endings

-ch/-sh/-s/-x/-o → +es
watch → watches
-y → -ies: study → studies

⚠️ Irregular verbs

have → he has
be → he is
do → he does
go → he goes
