💡 Quick Reminder – How to form the Simple Present
▼

✅ Positive

I / you / we / they: verb
He / she / it: verb + s

I play. · She plays.

🚫 Negative

I / you / we / they: don't + verb
He / she / it: doesn't + verb

I don't play. · She doesn't play.

❓ Questions

I / you / we / they: Do ...?
He / she / it: Does ...?

Do you play? · Does she play?

⚠️ Special endings

-s/-sh/-ch/-x/-o → + es
consonant + y → -y + ies

watch → watches
study → studies
